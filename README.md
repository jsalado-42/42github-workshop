## Experience

Since I began my development journey in 2017, I have primarily worked on Frontend and Backend (also known as FullStack) working mainly with HTML, CSS, Javascript and their most used frameworks (React, Angular, Vue, Express, etc).

Additionally, I have experience in many other languages such as Java, Python, C# or PHP. Also, I love working with best practices as design patterns (Clean Architecture, MVC or DDD). Most recently, I have designed, developed and maintained several APIs (REST, GraphQL, Web Scraping with XML and JSON) and create data processors (ETL).

I am also familiar with UX/UI (Figma, Penpot and XD), database management (MySQL, MariaDB, Postgres, MongoDB), version control (Git), and DevOps tools (Github Actions, Azure Pipelines, Jenkins, Docker, Kubernetes)

I have had the opportunity to work in a wide range of industries, including finance, telecommunications, insurance, tourisms, e-commerce, and even more niche sectors like weddings, fitness and barber shops!
